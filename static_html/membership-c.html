Common Questions

Quick answers. No ambiguity.

Who can join?
Finance and legal professionals with 5+ years of post-qualification experience, practising anywhere in the world. Both independent practitioners and employed professionals are welcome.
How long does approval take?
Every application is reviewed by a real person. You'll receive a decision within 5 business days. If accepted, your profile goes live within two weeks.
Do you take a commission?
Never. 100% of every client fee goes directly to you. Expertly charges a flat annual membership fee and nothing else — no cuts, no transaction fees, no hidden charges.
Can I cancel?
Yes, anytime. There's no minimum term or lock-in. If you cancel, your membership runs to the end of the billing period. No questions asked.
