How it works · From application to first client

Four steps.
Roughly two weeks.

We built Expertly as the antithesis of legal marketplaces: no race-to-the-bottom pricing, no commission on fees, no faceless middlemen. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you apply.

01
Apply

Submit your application.

Tell us about your practice, credentials, and specialisations. We review every single application — no bots, no auto-filters. Takes about 10 minutes.

  • Basic credentials & qualifications
  • Practice areas & jurisdictions
  • Two peer references (members or trusted counsel)
02
Verify

Get verified.

Our team manually reviews every credential: bar admissions, professional registrations, firm history, and published work. Peer references are contacted personally.

  • Credentials cross-checked with issuing bodies
  • Peer references contacted directly
  • Typical turnaround: 5 business days
03
Onboard

Build your presence.

Once approved, you'll get access to publish your profile, set rates, list availability, and start publishing articles. Our onboarding team is on call.

  • Custom profile page on expertly.network
  • Set rates, minimums & availability
  • Access to member events & community
04
Match

Get discovered.

Clients find you via AI-powered search, direct browsing, or article discovery. Every enquiry routes directly to you — we never sit in the middle.

  • AI search surfaces your expertise
  • Direct consultation requests in your inbox
  • Zero commission on any engagement
Why this approach

We're not a marketplace. And we don't want to be.

Traditional Legal Marketplace
  • ✕ Race-to-the-bottom pricing wars
  • ✕ 15–30% commission on every engagement
  • ✕ Anonymous profiles, no vetting
  • ✕ Platform sits between you and client
  • ✕ Your work feeds their brand, not yours
  • ✕ Clients churn to cheapest bidder
vs
Expertly Membership
  • Transparent, self-set rates
  • Zero commission — ever
  • Manually verified credentials
  • Direct relationship with client
  • Your profile, your brand, your IP
  • Clients stay because of you
Section 06 · Trust

What Verified actually means.

Our verification is selective by design. We'd rather reject ten good applicants than admit one we can't vouch for.

01

Credentials reviewed

02

Peer references

03

Good standing

04

Transparent rates
