How it works · From application to first client

We built Expertly as the antithesis of legal marketplaces: no race-to-the-bottom pricing, no commission on fees, no faceless middlemen. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you apply.

01
Apply

Submit your application.

Tell us about your practice, credentials, and specialisations. We review every single application: no bots, no auto-filters. Takes about 10 minutes.

02
Verify

Get verified.

Our team manually reviews every credential: bar admissions, professional registrations, firm history, and published work. Peer references are contacted personally.

03

Build your presence.

Once approved, you'll get access to publish your profile, set rates, list availability, and start publishing articles. Our onboarding team is on call.

  • Access to member events & community

Match

Get discovered.

Clients find you via AI-powered search, direct browsing, or article discovery. Every enquiry routes directly to you. We never sit in the middle.

  • AI search surfaces your expertise
  • Direct consultation requests in your inbox
  • Zero commission on any engagement

FAQ

Straight answers.

Does Expertly take a cut of my consultation fees?+

No. Ever. We're a membership platform, not a marketplace. You pay a flat annual fee; every dollar a client pays you, you keep. We don't mark up rates or take commission.

Can I try it before committing to a year?+

Yes. Monthly billing is available ($49/mo Budding, $69/mo Seasoned) and you can cancel anytime. Annual billing saves 17%.

What if I upgrade from Budding to Seasoned mid-year?+

We pro-rate the difference. If you cross 12 years of experience mid-membership or want the premium features, you only pay for the time remaining, no double-charging.

Are there any hidden fees?+

None. No listing fees, no featured-placement upsells, no per-consultation charges. The annual or monthly fee is it.

What's the refund policy?+

Full refund within 14 days of purchase, no questions asked. After that, we refund on a pro-rated basis at our discretion.
